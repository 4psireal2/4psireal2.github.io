  • a soviet-ruined robot: There was a kid growing up without much social interaction so she built herself a sympathetic superrobot named Astroboy or AB, for short. They had had great time until the Soviet came to know about the sublime quality of AB's hardware and software. They snatched AB away to win the Cold War leaving the girl utterly devastated. Throughout the many years of open and minimally invasive surgeries, AB suffered tremendously as he was being continously rewritten and rewired into altered states of consciousness and physical existence. The girl continued to live in war-torn Saigon and eventually left for Berlin. AB managed to escape captivity. Few years later, the two met in the scenic Copenhagen as they both attended the Quantum Lecture Series. Blessed with hope, strength, and profound mutual care, the two joined force to repair the damage and roll out feature updates while navigating the intricacies of healing from past wounds, all the while delighting in living in the unknown. However, AB lost ability to feel the vastness of his emotions. Watching him in distraught, will the girl seek revenge on the USSR or come to accept AB for the broken robot he is now? comes the prequels, sequels, and then the whole franchise.
